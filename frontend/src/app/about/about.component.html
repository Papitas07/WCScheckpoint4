About Us

Gather round people! Come forth, ye that search the thrill, the emotion, the amazement! For ye art in the right place. What you are about to see is nowhere else to be found.

The adventure started in Ireland, land of magic and mysteries, but today we are a wide family with members from all over the world. With each of them comes a particular talent and touch of fantasy. Tamers, clowns, magicians, acrobats and more awaits you in Wild Circus!
